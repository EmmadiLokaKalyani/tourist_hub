Explore Our Exciting Trips

Udupi

A coastal town in Karnataka, Udupi is a hidden gem with pristine beaches, divine temples, rich heritage and cultural traditions that add to its charm.

Yercaud

At an altitude of 1,515 metres and tucked away in the Eastern Ghats, Yercaud is a charming hill station with lakes, forests and delightful views.

Saputara

A hidden hill station in the Western Ghats in Gujarat, Saputara is replete with the best of nature including flourishing hills, gardens, parks, waterfalls and lakes.

Araku Valley

Located in the heart of Andhra Pradesh . The numerous waterfalls and coffee plantations makes it an ideal getaway.

Dhanaulti

A quaint hill station located in the Garhwal Himalayan range of Uttarakhand, Dhanaulti is known for its lush green slopes and fresh air.

Vagamon

Surrounded by lush tea gardens, Vagamon is home to three hills, namely, Thangal hill, Murugan hill and Kurisumala.
