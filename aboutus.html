About us

Tourist Hub is a vibrant travel and tourism website founded by CEO Emmadi Loka Kalyani, in partnership with Gundlapally Akshaya, Thota Deekshitha, and Dandeboina Mamatha. With a combined age range spanning from early twenties to late thirties, the team brings a dynamic blend of youthful energy and seasoned expertise to the forefront. Dedicated to exploring the world's most captivating destinations, Tourist Hub provides a comprehensive platform for travelers of all ages to discover new adventures, plan itineraries, and find accommodation options suited to their needs.

Our Mission

Our mission at Tourist Hub is to provide personalized recommendations of tourist destinations based on people's age, ensuring our platform is incredibly useful and beneficial. By understanding the diverse needs and preferences of travelers across different age groups, we aim to offer tailored suggestions that cater to their interests and travel aspirations. Whether you're a young adventurer seeking vibrant nightlife and adventurous activities, a family looking for kid-friendly attractions, or retirees interested in serene getaways, Tourist Hub strives to curate insightful information that enhances your travel planning experience.

Meet The Founders

Emmadi Loka Kalyani, our visionary CEO, leads with innovative strategies to create a comprehensive travel resource. Gundlapally Akshaya contributes invaluable knowledge in travel logistics and customer service, ensuring seamless user experiences. Thota Deekshitha's proficiency in digital marketing and content creation enriches our platform with engaging and informative content. Dandeboina Mamatha's background in technology and user experience design guarantees an intuitive and user-friendly Tourist Hub platform. Together, we are dedicated to empowering travelers with personalized insights and reliable resources, making exploration and discovery accessible to all.
